Simple to start

How it works

From opening the app to mastering the concept — four steps, no setup, no login required.

01

Choose a tool

Browse the tools above and pick the lesson or activity your teacher has assigned — or explore on your own.

02

Enter your name

Type your first name to start. No account, no password, no email. Your teacher sees you join instantly.

03

Learn interactively

Answer questions, manipulate graphs, draw on the whiteboard, and earn XP for every correct answer.

04

Get real-time help

Raise your hand when you're stuck. Your teacher sees it immediately and can send you a hint or message.

Why it works

Designed for real learning

Every design decision was made to close the gap between online and in-person teaching.

⚡

Instant feedback

Every wrong answer shows you exactly what to check — not just a red cross. You learn from mistakes, not just correct answers.

🔗

Three representations

Table, graph, and equation always visible together. You build connections, not isolated facts you'll forget next week.

🔒

Scaffolded unlocking

Each question unlocks the next. You can't skip the conceptual steps to get to the formula — understanding earns access.

🌍

Real-world context

Paul's walk, Derek's basketball score, Layla's data plan — maths that has meaning before it has a formula.

👁

Teacher sees everything

Your teacher watches live: who's active, who's stuck, who raised their hand. Nobody suffers in silence.

📦

No install needed

Open a link. Start learning. Works on any device with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop, school Chromebook.
